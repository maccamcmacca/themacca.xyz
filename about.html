Introduction:

I am a computing student from Scotland that emphasises minimalist design and practical outcome.

On this page you will find some of my hobbies and interests and some things about me that should give you an idea of what I like and why. If you find any of it interesting or would like to contact me regarding it please do so via the details found on the contact page.

Programmer 💻

All of my public projects can be found on my GitHub and GitLab pages.

I have experience with and consider myself competent with the following programming languages and tools:

  • C and C++
  • Git version control software
  • Linux system administration
  • Posix compliant Shell scripting

I am currently learning golang.

Hobbies 🎲

Music 🎸

Incase you couldn't tell from the website title, I am an avid fan of Black Metal. I also enjoy Classical and Synth.

I am a proponent of active listening instead of passive consumption.

I own an ever growing CD collection and a cozy audio setup which I may detail on this site at some point.

Video Games 🎮

Unfortunately I enjoy playing some video games and there is no excuse for it.

I am also a fan of the Warhammer 40,000 tabletop war game and the massively expansive sci-fantasy universe that is based on it. I have a Necron army and an assortment of units from various Space Marine chapters that I could never commit to.
